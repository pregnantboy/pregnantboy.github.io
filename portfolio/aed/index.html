Description

Chance of survival for cardiac arrest decreases by 7-10% for every minute of delay in applying CPR or Automated External Defibrillator. However, there is currently no single source of AED location data available.

This app hopes to crowdsource the locations of all AEDs scattered around Singapore through gamification. Players can select between two arbitrary factions. The map is divided into multiple districts. To conquery a district, players have to seek out AEDs and capture them by taking a geo-tagged photograph. Once verified, a point will be awarded to their faction.
