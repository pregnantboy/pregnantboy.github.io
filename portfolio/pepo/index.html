Description

PEPO is a smart water tracking system that consists of a non-intrusive, clip-on sensor that sends real-time water usage data to an app. The sensor picks up when the shower is turned on or off and sends it realtime to the app, which calculates the volume of water used. The app tracks water usage based on a per-shower, daily, weekly and monthly basis. It includes an achievements page, weekly leaderboard and social challenge feature.
